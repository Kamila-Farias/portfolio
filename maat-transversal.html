Maat Transversal
Visual Identity
Academic Project
2023
Graphic identity for the new fictional cultural program of the Museum of Art, Architecture, and Technology (MAAT), MAAT TRANSVERSAL. The program includes short-term events in the areas of visual arts, performance, music, installation, video, and mixed media. It was designed for short-term events, covering various artistic areas, emphasizing transversality through dynamic graphics and visual overlays. With a vibrant color palette and contemporary typography, the visual identity provides engaging and sophisticated communication for the audience.
