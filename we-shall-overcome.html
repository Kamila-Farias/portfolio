We Shall Overcome
Poster Design
Academic Project
2022
Graphic Interpretation of ‘We Shall Overcome’ through the use of typography and shapes with the aim of creating a visual representation that conveys the timeless message of overcoming and solidarity present in the song. To meet the client’s unique communication style, four color variants of the poster were made.
