Blank Space
Editorial Design
Academic Project
2024
Blank Space is a manifesto that works with the exploration of white/empty space as a starting point for creativity, based on the book *Inside the White Cube: The Ideology of the Gallery Space* by Brian O’Doherty.
In addition to the book, the project has a flag that functions as an extension of the book.
