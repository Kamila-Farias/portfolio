Alfabeto Referências, Inspirações e Influências
Visual Identity
Academic Project
2023
Graphic support on the studio Experimental Jetset, translating into visual language the concept of the “Alphabet of References, Inspirations, and Influences” taken from the lecture at Design Indaba. The presentation aims to condense a wide range of references into just one minute for each, offering a concise and dynamic visual representation of each concept discussed.
