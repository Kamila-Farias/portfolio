How to be a Human Being if...
Visual Identity / Editorial Design / Conceptual Project
Academic Project in collaboration with Manu Putini, Maria Inês Martins and Louise Pinto.
2024
Project focused on humanity’s adaptation to the outside world as a form of well-being, addressing four layers in order to show this ad- aptation. The following layers were defi ned as - Identity, Material Possessions, The House, Collective Identity. Using as a starting point the work of HunderWasser - Five Skins - , as the author explained the connection between individual, natural and social environments as if they were five skins of the human being. Along with the editorial, we chose to develop an online platform where we give answers to questions about the human experience and the way we relate to the world around us. It’s an online catalogue of media that has speculated and stretched the possibilities of the human condition through fi ction. Using the four layers we we asked, how we would adapted if the layer in question would be disrupted.”
